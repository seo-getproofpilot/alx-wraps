Our Story

Born In TheWrap Bay

Queen Creek, Arizona

ALX Wraps started with a simple frustration: the shelves are full of car-care products made for paint, and almost none of them respect a wrap. Off-the-shelf soaps dull matte film. Cheap sprays leave matte panels blotchy. Aggressive cleaners lift edges and cut a wrap's life short.

So the ALX Wraps team built the line they wished existed — a complete, finish-safe system developed and tested on real installs, in the same bay where ALX wraps daily drivers, custom builds, and fleets across the Phoenix East Valley.

The Science

Built On Real Chemistry

Every finish, every film — here's what ALX care is built for, engineered and proven where the film actually lives.

pH-Neutral & Film-Safe

Balanced, wax-free chemistry that cleans hard but never strips finishes or attacks wrap adhesive.

Matte-Safe

Cleans and refreshes flat matte film without ever adding gloss or leaving a sheen.

Satin-Safe

Keeps satin finishes evenly soft — no blotching, no hot spots, no gloss creep.

Gloss-Safe

Deepens glossy wraps and PPF with streak-free clarity and zero haze.

UV & Oxidation Defense

Sealant layers shield against the sun that fades, chalks, and yellows unprotected film.

Tested In The Wrap Bay

Developed and proven on the exact wraps, PPF, and color PPF that ALX installs every day.

Hydrophobic By Design

Water sheets and beads straight off treated panels, so dirt rinses free and drying is faster.

Made For Wraps

Formulated around vinyl wrap — chemistry that respects the film and its edges.

Made For PPF

Tuned for paint protection film and color PPF — protecting the layer that protects your paint.
