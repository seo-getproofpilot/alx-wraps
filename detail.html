Born In TheWrap Bay

Queen Creek, Arizona

ALX Detail started with a simple frustration: the shelves are full of car-care products made for paint, and almost none of them respect a wrap. Off-the-shelf soaps dull matte film. Cheap sprays leave matte panels blotchy. Aggressive cleaners lift edges and cut a wrap's life short.

So Alex Rapp and his partner built the line they wished existed — a complete, finish-safe system developed and tested on real installs, in the same bay where ALX wraps daily drivers, custom builds, and fleets across the Phoenix East Valley.
